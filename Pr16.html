Personal Computer (PC) is a versatile electronic device designed for individual use in tasks like working, gaming, and internet browsing.. Common types include desktops, laptops, and all-in-one machines, typically composed of a CPU, RAM, and storage devices. These, often, run operating systems to support various applications, providing,generally, user-friendly interfaces, reliability, and, often, cost-effectiveness. 


A Personal Computer (PC) is a versatile electronic device designed for
individual use in homes, offices, and educational settings, allowing users to
perform tasks like word processing, internet browsing, gaming, and multimedia 
consumption.

Lenovo and Wikipedia describe PCs as coming in various forms, primarily 
desktops and laptops, powered by central components including a CPU, 
memory (RAM), storage, and input/output devices like keyboards and monitors.

Originating from the development of microprocessors in the 1970s, modern 
PCs have become essential for storing and managing large datasets and 
performing high-speed processing tasks.
